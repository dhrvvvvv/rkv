About Us

Welcome to RAMA KIDS VILLA! We are an English medium school affiliated to the UP board, offering classes from preschool to class 8. Our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where children can grow and learn through creative play and exploration. Our dedicated and experienced teachers are committed to helping each child reach their full potential. We invite you to learn more about our school and join our community of learners..
